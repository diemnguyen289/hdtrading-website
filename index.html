FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

Business information for prospective partners.

Do you sell directly to consumers?

No. HD Trading LLC is focused on B2B wholesale relationships.

Which product categories do you purchase?

Our primary focus includes vitamins, supplements, sports nutrition, protein, beauty, personal care, and OTC healthcare.

Do you have resale documentation?

Yes. Appropriate resale and business documentation is available to qualified suppliers upon request.

What information should suppliers provide?

Please provide your company website, catalog, brand list, wholesale price list, MOQ, payment terms, marketplace restrictions, and account requirements.

How quickly do you respond?

We generally respond to qualified purchasing inquiries within one to two business days.

Do you require a specific MOQ?

MOQ is evaluated by supplier, product category, pricing, and commercial fit.
